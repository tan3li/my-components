### Usage

> **Badges vs Tags**: Badges and [Tags](https://storybook.tan3li.com/?path=/docs/components-navigation-tag--docs) are visually similar, but tags are interactive and **badges are not**.

<img src="/images/badge/TagvsBadge.jpg" alt="table comparing Tags and Badges" />
